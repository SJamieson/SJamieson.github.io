Building AI that makes safe
decisions under uncertainty.

I’m Stewart Jamieson — an AI researcher and roboticist building uncertainty-aware machine learning: systems that know the limits of their own knowledge, and how to overcome them. At Themis AI, I lead the work to turn that research into AI the world can trust.

  • MIT Ph.D.

  • Workshop Organizer · ICRA / ICLR

For a decade, one fundamental question has run through everything I’ve built — how do we build AI that understands how much to trust its own answers? From uncertainty quantification for self-driving vision, to risk-aware online learning and Bayesian inference for autonomous robots, to commercializing that research as reliable, production-ready AI.
